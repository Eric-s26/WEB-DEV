    Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) is a distinguished private institution of higher learning located in Uganda, founded with a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.
    Established in 1993, UMU has grown into a reputable university known for its diverse academic programs, research initiatives, and strong community engagement.
    Rooted in the values of integrity, service, and innovation, Uganda Martyrs University aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to contribute meaningfully to society and the global workforce.
